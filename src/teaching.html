Teaching
Ben Valine teaches private lessons in guitar, banjo, violin, viola, and mandolin to both children and adults. Several of his former students have won various school and regional competitions in Minnesota. In addition to instruction on various instruments, Valine specializes in teaching improvisation and applied music theory. Specifically, he has helped classical musicians utilize their technique in improvisatory settings. Valine has been a private instructor since 2008, and currently teaches beginning theory and aural skills at Belmont University as a graduate student.
